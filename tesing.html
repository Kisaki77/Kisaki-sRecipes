How to make a homemade Pizza!

Pizza

This pizza recipe is the best recipe you'll need to make homemade pizza. It only takes 45 minutes to cook and it's super easy. It just need few simple ingredients and any kind of ingredients to your liking may be added too. Serve pizza hot with any drink for either lunch, supper, or a snack.

Pizza Ingredients

Here are the ingredients that you'll be needing for this recipe.

  • Flour:

    • 1 cup of all-purpose flour.


  • Salt:

    • A pinch of salt.


  • Tomatoes:

    • 2 medium sized tomatoes.


  • Onions:

    • 2 medium onions.


  • Cheese:

    • 1 bar cheese.


  • Garlic:

    • Garlic makes it have a strong flavour.


  • Greenpepper, Redpepper and Yellowpepper:

    • 1 of each peppers.


  • Instant Yeast:

    • 1 tablespoon of instant yeast.


  • Mince Meat:

    • Any mince meat of your liking.


  • Chicken:

    • Chicken breast.


  • Water:

    • 1 litres of water.


  • Cooking Oil:

    • Half a cup of cooking oil.


  • Butter:

    • 1 gram of butter.


  • Any Toppings of your liking

Instructions

1. Take a bowl and pour the cup of all-purpose flour, add a pinch of salt, a tablespoon of instant yeast and a cup of water then mix well.

2. Take a wooden serving board and a roller, put a tablespoon of flour all over the board, put the dough and use the roller to flatten the dough.

3. Take pizza pan or an oven roasting pan, put butter all over the pan then take the pizza dough and lay it on the pan. Take a fork, make few holes on the dough and make a roll at the edges towards the centre then cover the pan with a kitchen cloth or plastic wrap and leave for 30 minutes.

4. Take the chicken breast and cut it into small pieces then put it in a pot, add a pinch of salt, a bit of water and cook for 15 minutes. Take the mince meat put it in a pot, add a pinch of salt, a bit of water and cook for 15 minutes.

5. Make a tomato soup: Chop 1 onion, chop the garlic and put them into a pot. Add a bit of cooking oil and let them fry until they turn brown. Take 1 tomato, remove it's skin, chop into small pieces and put inside the pot, add a pinch of salt and your favorite spices then let it cook for 5 minutes.

6. Chop 1 onion, peppers and 1 tomato into small square pieces and put them inside a bowl.

7. Take the cheese then grate it and put it in a bowl.

8. Take the tomato soup and out it all over the dough in the pan, add the cooked chicken breast and mince meat, put them all over the dough, add the chopped tomato, onions and peppers on top of the meat, take the grated cheese and add it on top then put the pan in an oven and cook for 15 minutes.
